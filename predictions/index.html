Food Inspection Predictions: August 2014

The algorithm developed by the City of Chicago estimates the chance that a restaurant will have a critical violations. The chances, which range from 0 to 1 and represent the "probability" of critical violations, are then used to prioritize canvas inspections. This map shows the estimates probability for restaurants in August 2014.

These predictions were generated from the sample data and code that is available here.
